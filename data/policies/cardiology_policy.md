METROPOLITAN HEALTH INSURANCE CORP -- CLINICAL GUIDELINE: CATH-004
SUBJECT: CARDIAC CATHETERIZATION & CORONARY ANGIOGRAPHY
EFFECTIVE DATE: JANUARY 01, 2026
========================================================================

1.0 DESCRIPTION:
This document outlines the clinical coverage criteria for diagnostic left heart 
catheterization and coronary angiography in adult patients.

2.0 REQUIRED CLINICAL DOCUMENTATION FOR AUTHORIZATION APPROVAL:
To achieve a 100% compliance score for authorization, the submission MUST contain:

  A) Symptom Documentation: Evidence of ischemic chest pain (angina) refractory 
     to medical management, OR stable angina with a limiting lifestyle impact.
  
  B) Objective Diagnostic Testing: A positive non-invasive stress test 
     (Exercise Stress Test, Stress Echo, or Nuclear Stress Test) demonstrating 
     reversible ischemia.
  
  C) Laboratory Work: A recent Serum Creatinine clearance level drawn within 
     the last 30 days (to evaluate contrast allergy/renal risk).
  
  D) Specialist Consultation: A formal cardiology evaluation note signed by 
     a board-certified cardiologist recommending the procedure.

3.0 EXCLUSIONS:
Requests submitted without a prior non-invasive stress test will be automatically 
marked as PENDING information request, unless the patient presents with an acute 
ST-elevation myocardial infarction (STEMI).
