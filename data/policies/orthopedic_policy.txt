========================================================================
APEX CARE NETWORKS -- MEDICAL COVERAGE POLICY: ORTHO-882
SUBJECT: TOTAL KNEE ARTHROPLASTY (TKA) / KNEE REPLACEMENT
EFFECTIVE DATE: MARCH 15, 2026
========================================================================

1.0 CLINICAL INDICATION:
Total knee arthroplasty is considered medically necessary for severe knee joint 
degeneration resulting from osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, or post-traumatic arthritis.

2.0 MANDATORY CRITERIA FOR APPROVAL:
Full authorization clearance requires validation of all four tracking items:

  A) Radiographic Evidence: X-ray or MRI reports within the past 6 months 
     confirming "severe joint space narrowing", "osteophytes", or "subchondral sclerosis".
  
  B) Conservative Management: Documented failure of at least three (3) months of 
     non-surgical treatment (including physical therapy, NSAIDs, or intra-articular 
     corticosteroid injections).
  
  C) Functional Impairment: Clinical notes establishing severe pain and functional 
     limitations that interfere with activities of daily living (ADLs) like walking or climbing stairs.
  
  D) Surgical Recommendation: An orthopedic surgeon's pre-operative assessment 
     confirming the patient is a clear candidate for structural reconstruction.
